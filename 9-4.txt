Internet
  - Composed of hosts (servers), communication links (e.g fiber), and
    packet switches (routers/switches that forward packets through network
    from incoming link to outgoing link)
  - Hosts are also called end systems because they are at the ends of the network
  - Nuts and bolts view
    - Network of networks
    - Governed by protocols, which are created by Internet Standards (through IETF)
